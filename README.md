# AI Content Lab

AI Content Lab is a comprehensive AI-powered platform integrated with Web3 technology, designed to provide a seamless experience for generating high-quality content, images, and videos.

## Key Advantages

*   **Comprehensive AI Suite:** Access a wide range of AI tools including Content Lab, Image Studio, Video Studio, and Design Studio.
*   **Web3 Integration:** Securely connect your MetaMask or OKX Wallet to manage your credits and transactions on the GenLayer Testnet.
*   **Credit System:** Simple and transparent credit system. 1000 Credits = 1 GEN token. New users get 100 free credits!
*   **Real-time Generation:** Fast and efficient content generation powered by Google's Gemini AI.
*   **User-Friendly Interface:** Modern, responsive design built with React and Tailwind CSS.

## GenLayer Testnet Details

The platform operates on the GenLayer Testnet. Use the following details to configure your wallet:

*   **Network Name:** GenLayer Testnet
*   **RPC URL:** https://rpc.testnet.genlayer.com
*   **Chain ID:** 4221
*   **Currency Symbol:** GEN
*   **Block Explorer:** https://explorer.testnet.genlayer.com

## How to Use the Site

1.  **Sign In:** Click the "Sign in with Google" button to create an account or log in.
2.  **Connect Wallet:** Click "Connect Wallet" in the sidebar to link your MetaMask or OKX Wallet. The app will automatically prompt you to switch to the GenLayer Testnet.
3.  **Get Credits:** New users start with 100 free credits. To get more, click "Buy Credits" in the sidebar and follow the instructions to pay with GEN tokens.
4.  **Select a Tool:** Choose a category from the sidebar (e.g., Content Lab) and select a specific tool (e.g., Blog Post Generator).
5.  **Generate Content:** Fill in the required fields and click "Generate". Each generation costs 10 credits.
6.  **Download/Copy:** Once generated, you can copy the text or download the images/videos to your device.
